About the Ryanair Junior Department

The Ryanair Junior Department is an exclusive programme designed for young aviation enthusiasts aged 10-16. Participants travel from London Stansted to our Dublin HQ to experience a day filled with exciting activities, including:

  • A behind-the-scenes tour of Ryanair's operations centre.
  • Flight simulator sessions where you can "pilot" a virtual Ryanair aircraft.
  • Team-building challenges and games organised by Ryanair staff.
  • Exclusive merchandise and a Junior Pilot Certificate upon completion.

Membership Fees

  • Annual Membership: £1000 per year.
  • Waiting Line Fee: £5 per person (non-refundable).

All fees must be paid upfront, and no refunds are available. As a budget airline, we pride ourselves on offering transparent pricing for these unique opportunities.
